FAQ

  1. Where can I purchase lotto tickets?
    You can buy tickets online through the official website or app, or at authorised outlets including kiosks, newsagents, and selected retail locations.
  2. What types of lotto games are available?
    Choose from large weekly jackpot draws, daily lotto games with fast results, or instant-win scratch tickets.
  3. How do I check if my ticket has won?
    You can log in to your online account, use the official app, or scan your physical ticket at participating retail stores to see your results.
  4. Are lotto winnings taxed?
    No. Lotto prizes are paid out tax-free to winners.
